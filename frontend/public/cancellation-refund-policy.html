Cancellation and Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At Freshwala, we understand that plans change. This policy outlines our cancellation and refund procedures to ensure a fair and transparent process for all customers.

1. Order Cancellation

1.1 Cancellation by Customer

Order Status Cancellation Allowed Refund Amount
Order Placed (Not Packed) ✓ Yes 100% Full Refund
Order Packed (Not Dispatched) ✓ Yes 100% Full Refund
Order Dispatched ✗ No Not Applicable
Out for Delivery ✗ No Not Applicable
Quick Cancellation: You can cancel your order anytime before it's dispatched. Simply go to "My Orders" and click "Cancel Order".

1.2 How to Cancel Your Order

Via Website/App:

  1. Login to your account
  2. Go to "My Orders"
  3. Select the order you want to cancel
  4. Click "Cancel Order" button
  5. Select reason for cancellation
  6. Confirm cancellation

Via Customer Support:

  • Call: +91 91722 20033
  • WhatsApp: +91 91722 20033
  • Email: support@freshwala.online

1.3 Cancellation by Freshwala

We may cancel your order in the following situations:

  • Product is out of stock or unavailable
  • Delivery address is beyond our service area
  • Technical issues with payment processing
  • Suspected fraudulent activity
  • Force majeure events (natural disasters, emergencies, etc.)

In such cases, you will receive a full refund within 5-7 business days.

2. Refund Policy

2.1 Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a refund if:

  • ✓ You cancelled the order before dispatch
  • ✓ We cancelled the order due to unavailability
  • ✓ You received wrong items
  • ✓ You received damaged or spoiled products
  • ✓ You received items of inferior quality
  • ✓ Order was not delivered within promised timeframe (after 48 hours delay)

2.2 Non-Refundable Situations

No refunds will be provided for:

  • ✗ Orders already delivered and accepted
  • ✗ Change of mind after delivery
  • ✗ Minor variations in product appearance (natural for fresh produce)
  • ✗ Delivery charges (unless order was cancelled by us)
  • ✗ Orders cancelled after dispatch

2.3 Refund Process Timeline

Stage Timeline
Cancellation Request Immediate
Refund Initiated Within 24 hours
Refund Processing 5-7 business days
Amount Credited Depends on your bank (usually 3-5 days after processing)

2.4 Refund Methods

Refunds will be processed to:

  • Original Payment Method: If paid via card/UPI/net banking
  • Bank Transfer: If original method is not available
  • Freshwala Wallet: Option to keep credit in your account for future orders

3. Quality Issues and Returns

3.1 Reporting Quality Issues

If you receive products with quality issues:

  1. Contact us within 2 hours of delivery
  2. Provide order number and details
  3. Share clear photos of the items
  4. Describe the issue clearly

3.2 Resolution Options

For valid quality complaints, we offer:

  • Option 1: Full refund for affected items
  • Option 2: Replacement delivery (same day if possible)
  • Option 3: Store credit for future purchases

3.3 Quality Guarantee

We guarantee:

  • ✓ Fresh products matching quality standards
  • ✓ Correct items as per order
  • ✓ Proper weight/quantity
  • ✓ Hygienic packaging

If we fail to meet these standards, we'll make it right with a refund or replacement.

4. Partial Cancellations

You can cancel individual items from an order:

  • Only before the order is dispatched
  • Partial refund will be processed
  • Remaining items will be delivered as scheduled
  • If order value falls below minimum, delivery charges may apply

5. Failed Deliveries

If delivery fails due to:

  • Customer unavailability (after 2 attempts)
  • Incorrect address provided by customer
  • Customer refused to accept delivery

Action: Order will be cancelled and refund processed minus delivery charges.

6. Promotional Discounts and Refunds

  • If order is cancelled, discount/coupon will be credited back to your account
  • Refund will be for the amount actually paid (after discount)
  • One-time use coupons can be reused if order is cancelled by us

7. Special Circumstances

During peak seasons, festivals, or emergencies:

  • Refund processing may take additional 2-3 days
  • We'll notify you of any delays
  • Your refund is guaranteed regardless of delays

8. How to Track Your Refund

  1. Check refund status in "My Orders" section
  2. You'll receive email confirmation when refund is initiated
  3. Another email when refund is completed
  4. Check your bank account/payment method after processing time

10. Dispute Resolution

If you're not satisfied with the resolution:

  1. Escalate to our customer service manager
  2. We'll review your case within 24-48 hours
  3. Fair resolution will be provided based on our policies

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cancellation and Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. Please review this policy periodically.
