Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At Freshwala, we are committed to delivering fresh vegetables, fruits, and groceries to your doorstep quickly and safely. This policy outlines our shipping and delivery procedures.

1. Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to the following areas:

  • Mumbai and surrounding areas
  • Pune
  • Thane
  • Navi Mumbai

Please check your pin code at checkout to confirm if we deliver to your location.

2. Delivery Timeframes

Order Placed Expected Delivery
Before 10:00 AM Same day (4-6 hours)
After 10:00 AM Next day morning (8:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
Weekends Within 24 hours
Express Delivery Available: Get your order delivered within 2-3 hours (subject to availability and additional charges).

3. Delivery Charges

  • Free Delivery: On orders above ₹500
  • Standard Delivery: ₹40 for orders below ₹500
  • Express Delivery: ₹80 (delivered within 2-3 hours)

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is confirmed:

  • You'll receive an order confirmation email/SMS
  • Track your order status in the "My Orders" section
  • Get delivery updates via SMS/WhatsApp
  • Our delivery partner will call you before delivery

5. Delivery Process

Step 1: Order Confirmation - We confirm your order and start preparing it.

Step 2: Quality Check - All items are checked for freshness and quality.

Step 3: Packaging - Items are carefully packaged to maintain freshness.

Step 4: Dispatch - Your order is dispatched for delivery.

Step 5: Delivery - Our delivery partner delivers to your doorstep.

6. Delivery Instructions

You can provide special delivery instructions at checkout:

  • Preferred delivery time slot
  • Gate/door code or security instructions
  • Leave at doorstep (contactless delivery)
  • Alternative contact number

7. Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery fails due to:

  • Customer unavailable
  • Incorrect address
  • Refused delivery

Action: We'll attempt redelivery once. If second attempt fails, order will be cancelled and refund processed as per our refund policy.

8. Packaging Standards

  • Fresh Produce: Packed in breathable bags/containers
  • Dairy Products: Insulated packaging with ice packs
  • Frozen Items: Thermal packaging to maintain temperature
  • Fragile Items: Extra cushioning and protection

9. Quality Guarantee

We guarantee:

  • ✓ Fresh products as shown in images
  • ✓ Proper hygiene standards maintained
  • ✓ Correct weight/quantity as ordered
  • ✓ Undamaged packaging

If you receive products not meeting these standards, please contact us immediately.

10. Holidays and Special Days

  • Regular deliveries on all days except major national holidays
  • During festivals, delivery times may be extended by 24 hours
  • Advanced booking recommended during peak seasons
